Blooming

Welcome to Blooming!
In Blooming, you will enter a magical garden and explore the delicate cycles and ecological balance of the natural world. This unique game allows you to experience the fascinating interaction between the life cycle of flowers and the flow of energy. Each area is randomly assigned with flowers or fertilizer, and your task is to trigger the cycles and guide the growth and decay of the flowers. When flowers lack nutrients, they wither and transform into fertilizer, providing valuable nourishment to the surrounding flowers. However, when the nutrients around the flowers become excessive, they also wither due to nutrient overload, transforming into fertilizer. But the story doesn't end there...
Fertilizers also have their own cyclic life cycle. When the nutrients around the fertilizer are abundant, they burst into new life, growing into fresh blossoms. At the same time, flowers also spread as seeds, allowing the surrounding fertilizers to grow into new flowers.
In Blooming, you will face challenges and make decisions. Observe the quantity relationship between flowers and fertilizers, strategically position them to trigger the perfect cycle. Master the art of cycles, and you will witness the miracle of beautiful flowers growing, thriving, and spreading in the garden.
Are you ready to explore this garden full of cyclic mysteries? Dive into the world of Blooming now and discover the beauty of nature and the endless path of cycles!
