Project Explanation

Each of the flowers obeys four simple rules:

1. The flower is surrounded by ≥ 3 flowers, the flower will be undernourished and wilt, turning into fertilizer
2. The flower is surrounded by ≥ 4 pieces of fertilizer, the flower will be over-nourished and wilt into fertilizer
3. If 2 pieces of fertilizer around the fertilizer, the nutrients are sufficient and new flowers grow
4. If 3 flowers around the fertilizer, which grow into new flowers due to the spread of the flower seeds
